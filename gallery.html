Color Guard

The Color Guard of CAT is the group that carries and presents the flag during school ceremonies and special events. They show discipline, respect, and teamwork in every flag ceremony and activity.

House to House

House-to-house activities in CAT involve visiting homes to share information, conduct community surveys, or give assistance when needed. This helps students build communication skills, responsibility, and stronger connections with the community.

Activities

CAT has different activities like drills, leadership training, community service, school programs, and team-building exercises. These activities help students learn discipline, responsibility, and teamwork.

Open Album

Lakbay Sanay

Lakbay-Sanay is an activity where students travel to different places to learn new skills and gain real-life experience. It helps them become more independent, observant, and responsible while learning outside the classroom.

Open Album

Bautismo

For bautismo events, CAT members may assist in maintaining order, guiding guests, and helping with simple event tasks. This activity helps students practice responsibility, discipline, and proper behavior during special occasions.

Open Album

Budol Fight

Budol Fight is an activity where members eat together using banana leaves as a table and share food without plates. It promotes unity, teamwork, and bonding among members while practicing discipline and cooperation.

Leadership Training

Leadership training in CAT helps students develop confidence, responsibility, and good decision-making skills. It teaches them how to guide a team, communicate clearly, and become good role models to others.

Achievements

CAT achievements include winning competitions, completing successful training activities, and actively participating in school and community events. These accomplishments show the members’ discipline, teamwork, leadership, and dedication.
